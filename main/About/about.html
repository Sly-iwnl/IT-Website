About Us

Here at Sly's Custom Cars we specialise in custom muscle cars but we always love experimenting with new styles and types of cars. We would love to have you pop in and say hello, we can even have a look at your car for free and we can see what types of mods we can do and how much it will cost. Come in and see our team today.
